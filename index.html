Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

The church is a holy place for the students, especially the Jewish and Chrisitains. Here, Jews pray to Jesus and sometimes use this quiet room as a place to think or calm down



School Library

Was built in 1980

The library has a large selection of children`s most beloved books, and furniture such as counters, tables, and chairs to hang out comfortably. The decor is quite colorful and cheerful-looking, as shown in the photo above.

School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

The auditorium is like a big theater where big announcements, assemblies, and performances by the choir, orchestra, band, or a proffesional group paid to perform take place. The seats are well-cushioned and quite comfortable. The stage has plenty of space for performances, and the lighting system is also very good.
